Our Retained Search Process

Our methodical six-step approach ensures we identify and secure the most qualified executive talent for your organization's leadership needs.

1. Client Consultation

We begin with an in-depth discovery session to understand your business goals, organizational culture, and specific leadership requirements. This collaborative approach allows us to develop a comprehensive position profile that aligns with your strategic objectives.

2. Market Mapping

Our research team conducts exhaustive market analysis to identify top-tier talent across relevant industries and geographies. We map the competitive landscape to uncover hidden talent pools and emerging leaders who could drive innovation in your organization.

3. Candidate Engagement

We discreetly approach potential candidates with a compelling narrative about your opportunity. Our consultants build relationships with executives, assessing their motivations, career aspirations, and potential cultural fit with your organization.

4. Assessment & Shortlisting

Candidates undergo our rigorous evaluation process, including structured interviews, situational judgment assessments, and optional psychometric testing. We evaluate technical skills, leadership capabilities, and cultural alignment to identify the strongest matches for your role.

5. Presentation & Placement

We present a diverse shortlist of exceptional candidates with detailed assessment reports. Our consultants facilitate the interview process, gather feedback, and guide negotiations to secure your preferred candidate under optimal terms for long-term success.

6. Post-Placement Support

Our commitment extends beyond placement with structured onboarding support and regular check-ins during the crucial first months. This ensures seamless integration and sets the foundation for lasting executive success within your organization.

Retained Search FAQs

What is retained executive search?

Retained executive search is a premium recruitment methodology where clients engage our firm exclusively to identify and secure high-caliber executive talent. Unlike contingent recruitment, this partnership-based approach involves a dedicated commitment from our consultants, comprehensive market mapping, and a guaranteed outcome.

How does retained search differ from contingent search?

Retained search involves a consultative partnership where we work exclusively on your role with a guaranteed delivery timeline. It features a more thorough market assessment, deeper candidate evaluation, and complete confidentiality. Contingent search typically involves multiple agencies working without exclusivity and focuses on active job seekers rather than comprehensive market mapping.

What is the typical timeline for a retained executive search?

A comprehensive retained executive search typically takes 8-12 weeks from engagement to offer acceptance. This timeline includes the initial discovery and position profiling (1-2 weeks), market mapping and candidate identification (2-3 weeks), assessment and shortlisting (2-3 weeks), and client interviews and selection (2-4 weeks). Our structured approach ensures efficiency without compromising quality.

What positions are best suited for retained search?

Retained search is ideal for C-suite positions (CEO, CFO, CIO, CTO, COO, CHRO), senior leadership roles (SVP, VP), specialized technical leadership positions, and strategic hires critical to business success. It's particularly valuable for roles requiring deep industry expertise, cultural fit assessment, or when confidentiality is paramount.

How do you ensure candidate quality in retained search?

Our rigorous assessment methodology evaluates candidates across multiple dimensions: technical capabilities, leadership competencies, cultural alignment, and strategic vision. We employ structured interviews, case studies, psychometric assessments, and thorough reference checks. Each candidate presented has been comprehensively evaluated against your specific requirements and organizational culture.

What is your geographic reach for retained executive search?

Carver Cross International conducts global executive searches with particular strength in Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific markets. Our London and Spain offices serve as hubs for our international research capabilities, allowing us to identify exceptional leadership talent regardless of location. We've successfully completed searches across 27 countries in the past three years.
