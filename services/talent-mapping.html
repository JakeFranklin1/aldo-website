Our Talent Mapping Process

Our systematic four-phase approach delivers comprehensive talent intelligence tailored to your specific strategic objectives and market context.

1. Strategic Scoping

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your strategic objectives, target markets, competitor landscape, and specific intelligence requirements. This framework ensures our research delivers precisely the insights you need to inform your talent strategy.

2. Comprehensive Research

Our research team conducts in-depth analysis using proprietary databases, industry networks, and market intelligence tools to map organizational structures, identify key talent, assess skill distributions, and benchmark compensation across your target landscape.

3. Data Analysis & Interpretation

We transform raw data into actionable intelligence through expert analysis, identifying patterns, opportunities, and threats in the talent landscape. Our consultants apply industry expertise to interpret findings within your specific business context.

Our consultants present findings to your leadership team, providing context and answering questions to ensure you can implement effective talent strategies based on the intelligence gathered.

Talent Mapping FAQs

What is talent mapping?

Talent mapping is a strategic intelligence service that provides organizations with detailed analysis of talent landscapes across specific industries, functions, or geographic regions. It delivers insights into competitor team structures, skill availability, compensation benchmarks, and emerging talent pools to inform strategic workforce planning and targeted recruitment initiatives.

How can talent mapping benefit my organization?

Talent mapping provides competitive intelligence to strengthen hiring strategies, identifies hidden talent pools before competitors, enables data-driven workforce planning, establishes accurate compensation benchmarks, and supports succession planning by revealing potential external candidates for critical leadership roles.

How is talent mapping different from executive search?

While executive search focuses on identifying and recruiting specific candidates to fill immediate openings, talent mapping provides comprehensive market intelligence about talent availability without necessarily pursuing candidates. It delivers strategic insights about competitor organizational structures, compensation trends, and potential talent pools to inform long-term recruitment strategy.

What information is included in a talent mapping report?

Talent mapping reports typically include organizational charts of competitor companies, skill distribution analysis across target markets, compensation benchmarking data, identification of emerging talent clusters, diversity statistics, education and experience patterns, potential candidate profiles, and strategic recommendations for talent acquisition approaches.

How long does a talent mapping project take?

The timeline for a talent mapping project depends on its scope and complexity, but most projects are completed within 3-6 weeks. Focused projects targeting specific roles or smaller competitor sets can be completed more quickly, while comprehensive market analyses covering multiple regions or functions may require additional time.

Can talent mapping be combined with executive search services?

Yes, many organizations begin with talent mapping to gain market intelligence and then transition to targeted executive search once they've developed a clear understanding of the talent landscape. This integrated approach ensures recruitment efforts are strategically focused and based on comprehensive market insights.
