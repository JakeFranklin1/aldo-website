Carver Cross International provides a complete Talent Search service within a compressed time frame, with exceptionally high success rates across Contingent and Retained campaigns. Our search framework is based on identifying the top performing individuals across the market and actively headhunting them on behalf of our clients. Each candidate is assessed in accordance to the matrix below.

Innovation

Competencies and abilities relating to creation and design of new things.

  • Complex problem solving
  • System thinking
  • Creativity
  • Integration skills
  • Methodology assessment & creation

Leadership

Competencies relating to leading people and business operations.

  • People leadership
  • Financial management
  • Administrative responsibilities
  • Deal negotiation skills
  • Critical thinking
  • Strategic input and advisory

Communication

Competencies relating to varying methods of communication.

  • Virtual collaboration
  • Interpersonal communication
  • Presentation skills
  • Verbal and written communication

Emotional Intelligence

Assessment of individual's ability to act based on emotions with consideration for others.

  • Adjusting behaviors appropriately
  • Utilize information to ascertain appropriate emotions
  • Situational understanding
  • Self-awareness

Skills & Knowledge

Competencies related to Subject Matter understanding and system development.

  • Mathematical modelling
  • System development
  • Simulation and integration
  • Technical expertise
  • Industry-specific knowledge
